Purpose of Tango Sydney

To inspire, support, accelerate and empower students to overcome their perceived limitations and achieve their highest potential in a friendly, fun environment.

Argentine Tango is passionate, sensual, elegant, romantic, intuitive, improvised and heartfelt. It is created "in the moment" from a variety of basic steps and hence is a pure form of communication - without words - a moving meditation to music.

Experience the magic, relax, have fun, get fit, meet new people, and be emotionally uplifted!

Tango Sydney's Principal Instructor

Sandy Grant is a natural born teacher who has taught children and adults in a variety of areas (swimming, water polo, dance, English to foreign students, computing and business subjects to MBA level, personal development) since age 12. She is passionate about tango since her first classes in Buenos Aires in 1999. In 2001, she spent almost 7 months in Buenos Aires learning tango and dancing up to 10 hours a day, 7 days a week. Since then she has supplemented her training through workshops by interstate and international teachers. She has been teaching tango in and around Sydney since October 2001.
